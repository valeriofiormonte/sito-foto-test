About

She doesn't wait for the light —
she goes looking for it.

Vittoria Serafini is a Rome-based photographer known for a proactive, on-the-move approach: scouting locations at odd hours, chasing weather instead of avoiding it, and staying ready to shoot the moment before it becomes obvious.

Over a decade behind the lens, she's built a practice that moves fluidly between disciplines — still life one morning, a wedding the next evening — always shooting with the same instinct for the unposed, unrepeatable frame.
